⏱️ Application Boot & Rendering Lifecycle
1

Bootstrapping & Data Resolution

The application triggers boot(), which executes a parallel Promise.all fetching 5 JSON datasets. Once loaded, global configurations (e.g. timezone filters) are initialized.

2

Standings and Ranking Computations

For each group, computeGroup() dynamically tallies matches played, goals for/against, points, and ranks them per matchday. Ranks are determined using: Points → Goal Difference → Goals Scored → Head-to-Head.

3

Bracket Tree Decoration

In the knockout tab, buildBracketStructure() creates a binary tree starting from the Final. The application dynamically assigns the real qualified group winners/runners-up or prediction states to the leaves, propagating winners to higher rounds.

4

Visualization Render Engine

DOM renders occur. High-performance SVGs are rendered: group trends line charts in chart.js, the polar coordinates knockout layout in renderBracketWheel(), and comparison timelines in renderEditionsChart().
